Removing Duplicates:

	* Knowing Duplicates

		> data_frame.duplicated() - Boolean result

	* Removing Duplicates

		> data_frame.drop_duplicates()

		> data_frame.drop_duplicates(inplace=True)

	df.duplicated()

	df.drop_duplicates(inplace=True) > permanant deletion
